I am an undergraduate student studying computer engineering at University of Illinois. I am currently doing research with the [Automated Software Engineering Lab](https://sites.google.com/site/asergrp/) lab at UIUC, advised by Tao Xie and PhD student Wei Yang. My research interests are in security, program analysis, and software engineering. The current focus of my research is on improving automated test case generation using grammar inference. My previous experience has been in reverse engineering and vulnerability research. I am currently fully funded by CyberCorps Scholarship for Service NSF Grant.    
  
I will be spending the summer at MIT Lincoln Lab's Cyber Systems Assessments Group.
